Madame Tussauds

Madame Tussauds is the name of a bunch of different wax statue museums all around the world. One of them is located in Amsterdam, near de Dam. Madame Tussauds contains a bunch of wax statues from celebrities. For example, our king Willem Alexander, Beyonce and Thor.
